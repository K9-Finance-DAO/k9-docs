# Rewards

1. Click on "Rewards" in the center menu
2. On this page you can track your liquid staking reward history.

At the top of the page you will see the following information:

* **KNBONE BALANCE** - your total balance of knBONE tokens in the Ethereum and Shibarium networks.
* **BONE EARNED** - your total earned BONE rewards at liquid staking
* **AVERAGE APR** - average APR of your rewards.
* **KNBONE PRICE** - current price of knBONE.

In the list below you will see your history:

1. Transfer In - if you have made a BONE deposit or knBONE has been transferred to your wallet from a different wallet;
2. Transfer out - in case you have withdrawn BONE from the protocol or transferred knBONE to another wallet;
3. Rewards - if you have received a reward from the protocol.
   * **Date** - transaction date. Displayed based on UTC+0 time zone. Hovering the cursor will display the transaction time.
   * **Type** - transaction type (from those given above).
   * **APR** - your profitability (in case of protocol rewards).
   * **knBONE Balance** - wallet balance after the transaction.
   * **BONE Reward** - the amount of the reward from the protocol.
