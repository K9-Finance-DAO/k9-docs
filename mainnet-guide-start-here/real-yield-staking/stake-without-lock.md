# Stake without Lock

#### Stake

In this type of staking, your deposit is not limited in any way, you can make a deposit and withdraw KNINE at any time:

**Deposit KNINE for Staking**

1. Using the form on the left, you can create a new position:
   * "Amount" input field for entering the KNINE deposit amount;
   * Your current balance of KNINE tokens and the “Use max” button to deposit the entire amount;
2. Information on your new deposit:
   * APR - current APR of the KNINE staking protocol;
   * Multiplier - your deposit multiplier, the base value x1 is applied;
   * Your Deposit - the deposit amount you specified.
3. The form on the right displays your Real Yield Staking status:
   * Status - displays your status - whether there is a deposit or not;
   * Amount - the amount of your deposit. You’ve sent this amount from your wallet;
   * APR - current APR of the KNINE staking protocol;
   * “Withdraw” button - withdraw the deposit along with the accumulated reward.
   * Reward - the amount of your accumulated reward in BONE tokens;
   * Total Statistics - protocol statistics:
     * Stakers - number of Real Yield Staking participants;
     * Total Staked - the sum of all deposits in Real Yield Staking;
     * TVL - total value locked at Real Yield Staking.
4. After you have indicated the deposit amount, click on the “Stake” button and confirm the transaction in your wallet;
5. Wait for the transaction to complete. Popup window displays the transaction progress.\
   ![](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXeCPHYHUMRS9jpk5Z2l8XGn5QB64fns5MOU4w6r5DjKOOkfcmelR9YipXJtelarB8CPHv_a8pL35PLL_lQMXI4fcXL76tcrKsOt1R15g_a5o6F8f0NIw52SISiQBiOduBU7MGHjt8Oc7AY0RoWfjt1U55IF?key=UHJy3JQkr0xZIlPVeyb1AA)
6. Once the transaction is complete, the updated deposit amount will appear in the form on the right.

### Steps to Add More KNINE to Existing Non-locked Deposit

You can increase your KNINE deposit in the current position by entering a new amount in the "Amount" field and clicking the "Stake" button.

1. Enter the amount of the new deposit and click on the “Stake” button;
2. Confirm the transaction in your wallet and wait until the transaction is processed (the transaction status is displayed in a modal window):\
   ![](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXc6-jh1A0JAVD-FcMqD2m4cTLXdnbbPi4AP-83qJYtQR30rI7W3ic9LFuQowCy-Nj-OrYb1Jsfy4VOOmJeR5ZqttGGmBpTIHAFT4qTcFK8t21U-XY0_6c7-RRBn5Av0iQhrTJfMrHLhmEcq83SdA5EsSLVH?key=UHJy3JQkr0xZIlPVeyb1AA)

The new deposit will be added to the previous KNINE deposit amount. Wait a while and check that the BONE reward amount increases in accordance with new parameters of your deposit.
