# Stake/Unstake LP

1. Click on "Stake" in the center menu.

In this type of farming you can deposit your LP and receive esKNINE rewards and bonus rewards.

<figure><img src="https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXf_Ol7IfigATzjN-Lpul1Xr57sACkBak38KU29EjJZnX0fBr1OoFIex1GiAL1e_pt01Q1Gc01Jcfwn9kCUbEz-RKQv3dlCd8-SbnZtD0hWfE-fdBS3a_ijpEnFzrQoAztiraNCM1oQIx7ldgS-fEuzxxTbb?key=UHJy3JQkr0xZIlPVeyb1AA" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

The form on the left displays the general info about the farming pool:

* Liquidity pair
* APR - annual reward rate of the farming pool;
* Your LP balance for this pool;
  * link to Shibaswap to provide liquidity;
* Staked liquidity - USD-estimated value of the total LP amount staked to the farming pool;
* Main reward token - the reward token of the pool.

![](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXf_Ol7IfigATzjN-Lpul1Xr57sACkBak38KU29EjJZnX0fBr1OoFIex1GiAL1e_pt01Q1Gc01Jcfwn9kCUbEz-RKQv3dlCd8-SbnZtD0hWfE-fdBS3a_ijpEnFzrQoAztiraNCM1oQIx7ldgS-fEuzxxTbb?key=UHJy3JQkr0xZIlPVeyb1AA)

**Stake**

The form on the right allows you to deposit your LP. There you will find the following info:

* "Amount" input field for entering the LP deposit amount;
* Your current balance of LP tokens and the “Use max” button to deposit the entire amount;
* “Stake” button - to execute the deposit;
* Your farming status;
  * Staked amount - your total active deposits;
  * LP distribution (tokens inside provided liquidity);
* esKNINE Earned - your current rewards (available anytime);
  * Unstake - withdraw your deposit and earned rewards;
  * Claim all - claim all rewards from Stake and from Zap.

To make a deposit:

1. Enter the amount you would like to deposit;
2. Click “Stake” and confirm the transaction in your wallet;
   * First transaction will be the ‘approve’ to allow the contract to use the amount of tokens you have specified;\
     ![](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXdSRTgA3GYxUheqY45L4a86askarNneQA98g0Cv5CC08vsKz_ta9mlx3yyYPgRQiYWnrrFnn4G7RCV-Djf3VAH39O9DnPuhwFiGjBb0QesFAc6H91aclw2ytoSqamKVMQB-7pCPc80pYvIb-p8SsBzQfNnG?key=UHJy3JQkr0xZIlPVeyb1AA)
   * The second transaction will be the deposit itself. During this transaction your LPs will be deposited to the pool. The popup window will display the transaction progress.\
     ![](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXceC1nIfYR7nxx0AUMm7eblyfjWwVARKpV-VJ1Kg3CgCOdcSNT2fOpC3PoSJoXgmbXwQ7-CXtk14sxutKKcBpLsyXdNzvPf7_OINb_mMXcU7-orr_O9OBaf4sHCXPVoKlem71jrVVKPvNHE3JHw-GbsWag2?key=UHJy3JQkr0xZIlPVeyb1AA)
3. Once the transaction is completed, the popup will display the transaction status;\
   ![](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXcOsp_CNyz3PZ_Nc9kgn3gznlP6NcOescR5qFi3iimfNrclPEGXrEgVukYXnUiffxKfpLy1cT-l1xtox2QSW3d6PXBaUUXl9XG2oGsJdGCtDSomNjczIxxBKX53T6zulTYVOMeGHDFXQllJX1-O0AT8WuHQ?key=UHJy3JQkr0xZIlPVeyb1AA)

Your LP tokens are deposited, and you will start accumulating the rewards.

**Unstake**

To unstake the LP tokens and rewards, you need to:

1. Click “Unstake” button
2. Confirm the transaction in your wallet. The popup will display the transaction status:\
   ![](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXfh-MQk5kd3ON7RTJw39MrqSk92T4y-rh6XEVogxx-C0lIP3qt3SR_VOZ_DJa-ZdOpAfxqCsMBk9XraPGQsmn1YAaEByP1ga6EbOOOAgmcEtGyETVNfdEQVVgcjeHflGUUbNuF7HzxVbVPsMy8LJ97wFm-d?key=UHJy3JQkr0xZIlPVeyb1AA)
3. Once the transaction is completed, the popup will display the transaction status:\
   ![](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXdKzii9Lb48ZjJj3Mb7S0T6HkGJ1M9ZYh0y-f_5sw-kUQEsGkn3zADklZlzc63fQzcP3tQsOWKgNeaN5hRCWTjp3swE6i6vRuzgUUdiyL_NSuIpzBg749Uxc6JpfgcHQDqL4m1b2xd8a4iSymeBEB7RBtI?key=UHJy3JQkr0xZIlPVeyb1AA)

**Your LP tokens and the accumulated rewards are withdrawn to your wallet.**
