# Zap

1. Click on "Zap" in the center menu.

In this type of farming you can deposit your LP and receive esKNINE reward and bonus rewards in one simple transaction.

<figure><img src="https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXfhArSUxfmMJsZgyD_wOPDxtWLzJsly8gz6eEAPAA-mb_HJdHjvfokw-Ke4jqOIwAQKcd_eSMK9qv87crkyoZIyN89_SWjDuNWF92Oj5YuAjHat--mPD2kbn13pnDxWYhv07Mf1QSZE0iEPgnjTYT-XwMWH?key=UHJy3JQkr0xZIlPVeyb1AA" alt=""><figcaption></figcaption></figure>

**Zap**

The form on the right allows you to deposit 1 of the 2 tokens of the pair. There you will find the following info:

* "Amount" input field for entering the token deposit amount;
* “Select token” dropdown - to choose the deposit token;
* Your current balance of the selected token and the “Use max” button to deposit the entire amount;
* “Zap” button - to execute the deposit;
* Zapping fee
  * Your farming status;
    * Zapped amount - your total active deposits;
    * LP distribution (tokens inside provided liquidity);
* esKNINE Earned - your current rewards (available anytime);
  * Unstake - withdraw your deposit and earned rewards;
  * Claim all - claim all rewards from Stake and from Zap.

To make a deposit:

1. Enter the amount you would like to deposit;
2. Click “Zap” and confirm the transaction in your wallet;
   * First transaction will be the ‘approve’ to allow the contract to use the amount of tokens you have specified;\
     ![](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXf_Rt9N4Qpjua9xvHjfyU9Bcqw55Dj_5D3XbrZX-1u7s5xzMZrFrBFKn04vJsRolKS4DRBz3IwqN01G1t7bCl3_Kc8FR8p4YfKj2alL1S2R5aYBu7nlHVD9wLPTPYmimLuEb3E1BfOuZo4DFn021EmnOXAi?key=UHJy3JQkr0xZIlPVeyb1AA)
   * The second transaction will be the deposit itself. During this transaction your tokens will be partially swapped to provide liquidity. After that, the LPs will be deposited to the pool. The popup window will display the transaction progress.\
     ![](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXf3mRhXJnWZ9Kq6QOAH9jRjY3l6uA-bdZhMy6WJLR5lMw-n2BWvdb8rKdcpy3Ln-U_erSw_Zte0Kbde8P49Zoehf9xwfopQiT92cOLNMh3jqB3QB1P5ftvO9GUCur0V9OhzAA8bACWosIvImoSKCKVAwDg?key=UHJy3JQkr0xZIlPVeyb1AA)
   * Once the transaction is completed, the popup will display the transaction status:\
     ![](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXdIorbK_EkIBmh7HVuCy7XgbUtHkrv8p-fB7Mgga4xcqB4ghdZswmVcZmS4BcxLtQcwXJEG8JJGDF29tN921yvIVRe8viEeloRVTa_LecdjU9RwNYqt8kK0hqglkjKAoyxHWFMv5pOhwOhGNduJlN-5XHG6?key=UHJy3JQkr0xZIlPVeyb1AA)

Your tokens are deposited, and you will start accumulating the rewards.

**Unzap**

To unzap the deposited tokens and rewards, you need to:

1. Click “Unzap” button;
2. Choose the token you would like to receive. Previously provided liquidity will be converted to this token:\
   ![](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXdxuIfmo20lLPLSusrjpTVV-r-3QQ2enTKO_IJBCB4PxZeom8OdHf41SXDHPO-RUwxTVlXII7IXXhfem_S62n1RnxXQBNriwnZmtFk9C0AF0bgnTVy9Mr27bYiGynuB4zrwPbsY3oiUTf0wRQvccrPwTnYb?key=UHJy3JQkr0xZIlPVeyb1AA)
3. Click “Unzap” and confirm the transaction in your wallet. During this transaction:
   * Your previously deposited LP (see Zap) will be removed from ShibaSwap;
   * The received amount will be swapped to get the token that you have chosen (see above);
   * the tokens and the reward is transferred to your wallet;
4. The popup will display the transaction status:\
   ![](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXfcsxK99WpyhS28mb7QsMB7v_jv0PaKVmYBCecQZuWB_KAV2XUKC1Ohn29DNawiRTil99RbPqtHfaxMEDAgmrGQEcNQHnQPACoAdnWVZlbjroO_p3S1KApFo8grcqS8Nn5GnC9qhfSY3h5CuZ8Y3-7HDwRu?key=UHJy3JQkr0xZIlPVeyb1AA)
5. Once the transaction is completed, the popup will display the transaction status:\
   ![](https://lh7-us.googleusercontent.com/docsz/AD_4nXeTuU8abrVU3PW1xgyKlIIJt-kVO4M9SGbYjiIHxlzoXBzhdckn3TrSTQexOHVc1ZXAf1Qoj1V2UEnzVvT7vsaQYR6xad3YjuYm9fsmRo7YGMPdV8G_Qb_9ZZDe7Z4B4b1tIRevs6OpV1nw5u9wt9yWWHGr?key=UHJy3JQkr0xZIlPVeyb1AA)

Your tokens (that you have chosen) and the rewards are withdrawn to your wallet.
